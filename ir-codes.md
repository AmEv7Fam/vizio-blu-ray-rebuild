## Notes

* Due to separate lowercase and uppercase letters, Shift and Caps on remote cannot be intercepted by IR reciever.
* OK on front and Enter on back share same IR code, FFF20D.
* Color keys on front and back do not share same IR codes.
* Arrow keys on front and back do not share same IR codes.
